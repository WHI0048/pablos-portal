Welcome to Pablo's Portal

Choose what you want to play with: a physics sandbox with a tiny player, explosions, a laser pointer, or a neon snake game with screen wrapping and RGB candy power-ups that grant speed, score, and temporary invincibility.

Physics Sandbox

Draw solids, liquids, and gases. Spawn a tiny player that can walk and jump. Use the lighter to try to ignite a flame – it only starts 1 in 5 times. If it lights near propane or explosive gas, everything blows up with a pixel-burst explosion and chain reactions. Use the laser pointer to slice through solids (except mirrors), and bounce the beam off mirror blocks.

States of Matter Playground

Click and drag to paint. Use the dropdowns to choose types for each state of matter.

Neon Snake

Use WASD or Arrow Keys. The snake wraps around the screen edges. Don’t hit your own tail—unless you’re in candy mode. RGB candy fruit (1 in 10) makes the snake super fast, RGB, invincible for 1 minute, and adds +1 score every second.
